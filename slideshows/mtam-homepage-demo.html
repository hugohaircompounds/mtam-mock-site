Why Christine and Alex built this

Christine spent years being told there was one right way to do extensions — one brand, one method, one set of tools. She got certified, followed the steps, and still couldn't explain to a client why she was choosing a technique over another.

The turning point was realizing that the "right way" was just one method's way — not the technique's truth. The principles behind great extension work aren't owned by any brand.

Together with Alex, she built MoreThanAMethod because every stylist deserves to understand the why — not just memorize the how.
